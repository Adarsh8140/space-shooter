This directory will contain game assets like:
- Background images
- Sprite sheets
- Sound effects
- Music files

For a complete game, you would need to add:
1. background.png - A space background with stars
2. spaceship.png - Player's spaceship sprite
3. alien1.png, alien2.png, alien3.png - Different alien sprites
4. explosion.png - Explosion animation sprite sheet
5. laser.png - Laser beam sprite
6. powerup_shield.png, powerup_laser.png, powerup_speed.png - Power-up sprites
7. obstacle_bomb.png, obstacle_meteor.png - Obstacle sprites
8. shoot.wav, explosion.wav, powerup.wav - Sound effects
9. background_music.mp3 - Background music loop
